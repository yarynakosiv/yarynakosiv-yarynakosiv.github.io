Sasha Boole is a Ukrainian country and folk musician. This vinyl cover design features decorations from an antique wedding chest. Its rural romantic style reflects the spirit of music and sets a background for white song titles.

Alchemy is an album with a Psy-trance music set. The cover is a flashback to psychedelic 60s when music concerts and drugs were popular among youth and designers tried to visually express the feeling of tripping out.
