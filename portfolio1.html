People and Puppets is a tiny puppet theatre (only 40 seats) in the heart of Lviv, Ukraine. As the name suggests, actors interact with puppets on stage creating a unique experience for their audience. The repertoire is mainly for children, but they have a few performances for adults, including plays based on Dante and Shakespeare. Since 2015, People and Puppets have been hosting an annual international festival for puppet theaters.

Based in Lviv, Ukraine, People and Puppets theatre needed a website to show all the information about performances, tickets, and their annual festival. Young parents and grandparents who plan to see the performance with their children or grandchildren are the main users of this website. The theatre wanted to create a simple and welcoming design experience for them. Website interface is minimalistic with a lot of pictures, bold text, and easy navigation. It is responsive and flexible, so one have smooth experience on any device.
